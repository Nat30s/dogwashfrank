About Our Business

RID - A - FLEA Mobile Dog Wash provides top-notch grooming services for dogs in the Brisbane area.

With over 10 years of experience, we ensure that your furry friend is clean, happy, and healthy.

We use only natural products to provide the best care for your pet.

Full Wash

Complete wash with shampoo and conditioner.

Drying
